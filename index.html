iChronicle (Beta)
The Secure, Self-Hosted Life Journal
Github Login / SignUp
Advanced Encryption
All journal entries are secured using AES-128 bit encryption. This encryption occurs 100% on your device, meaning your entries are never sent over the internet unincrypted and only you can view them.
Free as in Freedom
iChronicle is Open-Source. You are free to view, modify, and redistribute the source code as much as your heart desires. Your also welcome to contribute to the main iChronicle repository.
Cross Device Sync
Because iChronicle is hosted your entries are instantly synced with across of your devices. Don't have an internet connection? Fear not! Entries can be submitted offline and are synced when you re-establish a connection.
Other Features:
Works Offline
Entries can be submitted without a conection to the internet
Geotagging
Entries can be tagged with your current location so you can easily remember where you were when you wrote the entry
Mobile
iChronicle is optomised for use on mobile phones. Take your journal everywhere!
Entry Tags
Entries can be tagged for quick and easy organization.
Export/Import
Your journal data can be exported into JSON. Allowing you to easily migrate to another journal.
